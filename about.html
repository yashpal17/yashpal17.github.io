About

25 years of crafting water solutions that Indian homes and hotels trust. Rooted in Pune, reaching every corner of India.

The Beginning

From workshop
to national brand.

Ambav was founded in 1998 by Mr. Suresh Patil in a small workshop in Pune's Pimpri Chinchwad industrial belt. What started as a two-person operation making brass fittings has grown into one of India's most trusted plumbing manufacturers.

Today our 80,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility employs over 450 skilled workers and produces more than two million units annually across four product lines.

Our mission remains unchanged: make water flow better in every Indian home.

Our Facility

World-class
manufacturing.

Our Pune factory runs CNC machining centres, a robotic welding line, in-house PVD coating, and a fully automated quality lab.

01

CNC Precision Machining

Computer-controlled lathes and milling machines deliver micron-level tolerances on every brass and stainless steel component we produce.

02

PVD & Chrome Finishing

In-house Physical Vapour Deposition and chrome plating plant delivering finishes that outlast standard plating by 5× in salt-spray tests.

03

3-Stage QC Testing

Every unit undergoes pressure testing, 96-hour salt-spray corrosion testing, and optical inspection before it leaves our facility.

04

Zero-Waste Water Recycling

Our plating plant recycles 95% of all process water. Sustainable manufacturing has been central to our operations since 2005.

05

Pipe Extrusion Plant

Dedicated CPVC and uPVC extrusion lines with wall-thickness laser monitoring ensure every pipe meets IS pressure class specifications.

06

R&D Innovation Lab

A 15-person R&D team developing water-saving valves, touchless technology, and next-generation smart-home plumbing systems.
